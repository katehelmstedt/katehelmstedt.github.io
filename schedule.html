Unit content

This is both your schedule for the semester, and where all the links to the materials are contained. Come here for slides, videos, workshop materials, assessment task sheets, and zoom links. The assessment timelines are in the schedule and also in their own timeline at the bottom of the page.

This unit has three branches:
1. Data visualisation practice
  • How do we make things?
  • How do we convert data to images?
Covered in lectures (videos linked with & slides linked with in the schedule below), practiced in workshops (material linked with below).
Examined in Project 2 and Problem Solving Tasks 2-6

2. Visual communication theory
  • How do we as STEM professionals get our messages across?
  • What are good and bad visualisation choices?
Covered in lectures, practiced in workshops.
Examined in Projects 1 and 2, and Problem Solving Tasks 1-6

3. R Programming
  • What is the best tool for getting this done?
  • Achievable practice that you can use forever
Covered in lecture 2, practiced in workshops.
Examined in Project 2, and Problem Solving Tasks 2-6
